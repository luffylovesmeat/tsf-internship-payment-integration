About Us
In the year 1978 Mr. Uddeshya Tiwari established The Preservation of History Foundation after witnessing the dire condition of museums and archeological sites around the world during his travells. Since the establishment of this Foundation we have gained more than 2,000,000 supporters and we have helped over 30,000 Museums and Archeological around the globe.
